UK road trips

Welcome to UK road trips, here you can plan your road trip around the UK

The Uk has over 1000 years of history, with a wide variety of places to visit.

Bamburgh castle

This castle has a history of over 1,400 years, and is one of the largest inhabited castles in the country. In 1894 The castle as bought by William George Armstrong to house his unique collection of artwork, ceramic and objet d'art.

Edinburgh Zoo

Home to the Royal Zoological Society of Scotland (RZSS), they are a wildlife conservation charity. Edinburgh Zoo was opened in 1913 by Thomas Gillespie, a lawyer that founded the RZSS in 1909

Alnwick gardens

The first garden was created by the 1st duke of Northumberland Hugh Percy and then further developed by future dukes. The garden has plants from all over the world, in a closed off section, there is the poison garden with some of the most deadly plants from all over the world.
